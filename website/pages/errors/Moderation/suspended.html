Account Suspended Permanently

Your account has been suspended permanently due to multiple violations or serious violations taken against your account. If you believe this was a mistake, please contact support. You are entitled to submit an appeal, but please note that appeals for permanent suspensions require sufficient evidence that the violations were not valid. Appeals without sufficient evidence will be denied.


Account Compromise cannot be appealed, since the account owner is responsible for their account. Please do not waste the Support Team's time with appeals that do not meet the requirements, as this will only delay the review of valid appeals. If your account was compromised, please secure your account and submit an appeal with evidence of the compromise.


Until the suspension is resolved, you cannot access the site.
